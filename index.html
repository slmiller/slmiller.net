Welcome to Sarah Miller's Portfolio

I am a 5th year Architectural Engineering student at The Pennsylvania State University. I will be graduating in May of 2014 with a focus in lighting & electrical design.

My love of design goes back many years. I began to learn how to use AutoCAD when I was only 12, and since then I've added many more architectural tools to my skillset. As a child, I was fascinated by architecture. And as I grew, I developed an inclination towards science and mathematics. Architectural Engineering is the perfect blend of disciplines that allows me to develop a broad knowledge base.

In the summer of 2012, I applied my electrical design skills while working with an MEP firm. This past summer, I focused on lighting design while working with an architecture firm. Both of these internships have given me unique opportunities to learn about the applications of Architectural Engineering.
